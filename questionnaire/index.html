A questionnaire about navigational assistive technologies for visually impaired people for PhD research

Introduction

I am Marziyeh Bamdad, a PhD student at the University of Zurich. With this questionnaire, we want to understand the experience and needs of people with visual impairment. More specifically, we focus on digital assistive technologies that help visually impaired people with indoor and outdoor navigation. Depending on your answers, there are between 14 and 18 questions in this survey. It will take between 10 and 15 minutes. This information will be only used for this Ph.D. research and will be destroyed after this study is complete.

I am grateful for your time and effort dedicated to filling in this questionnaire.
